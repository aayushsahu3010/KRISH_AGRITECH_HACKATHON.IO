about us

Kisan of India- a true friend of the farmers. A place where they will become more aware and learn new techniques of farming in order to increase their incomes and become prosperous. On this website, we will make farmers and livestock farmers aware of the various government policies and schemes related to farming. We will tell them about agricultural start-ups. From the website, they will know about the seeds of high yielding varieties of crops and details of new research and experiments from agricultural experts in India and abroad. We will guide the farmer about which tractor is better suited for their needs and why. Which machines will be useful for farming and how? How to make agricultural produce ready for market challenges? Where and how will they get a better market and better price for their produce?In a nutshell, Kisan of India is an initiative which will serve as a lifeline to the Indian farmer. It will enable a dialogue between the farmers. It will serve as a marketplace for them. It will be helpful to fulfil the needs of the families and children of the farmers. Our goal is to make Kisan of India as personal as the courtyard of our own house and a trusted partner to farmers on their digital journey.
